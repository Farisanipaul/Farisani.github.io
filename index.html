About Farisani

Ratshikombo Farisani Paul

Student at The Coding Ground

Born in (July 26 1995), Limpopo, South Africa. Farisani is the youngest of 2 other siblings. He grew up in the villages where technology was very inaccessible but after matric he moved to Johannesburg where he started learning about technology. One of his favorite Qoutes is as follows, "Where you come from is not your future". Because of this Qoute Farisani became motivated and started to pursue a career in programming during the year 2020. After a long time of self-learning he decided to apply for a coding bootcamp at The Coding Ground.

Early Life

Love for Solar Energy Systems

Growing up in an environment where there is no electricity in the villages or load shedding happens more often, he saw an opportunity to research about how solar systems work. He worked as an electrician to install light bulbs and other home electrical appliances. During this time that is when the love for solar systems grew. He worked as a solar installer for a company called Lamo Solar which is based in Riverside, Midrand, South Africa.
